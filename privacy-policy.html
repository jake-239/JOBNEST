Privacy policy

How JobNest handles marketplace data.

This page explains the kinds of account, job, message, wallet, and usage information a freelance marketplace may collect.

Information we collect

JobNest stores account details, profile content, job posts, messages, wallet records, and activity needed to run marketplace workflows.

How information is used

Data is used to authenticate accounts, show jobs, support messages, display dashboards, process wallet activity, and protect the platform.

Your choices

Users can update profile information, adjust settings, manage notifications, and contact support for account help.
