How do I choose between a client and freelancer account?

Choose client if you want to post jobs and hire talent. Choose freelancer if you want to browse work, apply, and showcase your portfolio.

Can I edit my profile later?

Yes. Open Profile to update your name, bio, skills, experience, portfolio, certificates, and contact information.

How do clients post jobs?

Clients can open Post Job, enter the title, description, category, skills, budget, deadline, attachments, and publish the listing.

How do freelancers apply?

Freelancers browse jobs, open a job detail page, review budget and skills, then use Apply Now to record an application message.

Does JobNest support wallet activity?

The wallet page includes balance, earnings, deposit, withdrawal, payment methods, PayPal, Stripe, bank, and transaction history sections.

Where do messages appear?

Messages appear in the Messages page and dashboard summary cards for each account type.

How do I contact support?

Use the Contact page for account help, hiring help, payments, or issue reports.
