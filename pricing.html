FAQ

Pricing questions.

Can I change plans later?

Yes. You can move between Basic, Medium, and Advanced as your hiring or freelance work grows.

Do all categories use the same prices?

Yes. Web Development, UI Design, Writing, Marketing, and Business Support all use the Free, $5, and $10 plan levels.

Are payments included?

The wallet interface is ready for PayPal, Stripe, bank, deposit, and withdrawal workflows.
